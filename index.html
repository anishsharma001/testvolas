About VOLAS

About Us.

VOLAS NexGen Marine is a premier global provider of specialized maritime technical services, delivering high-impact audits, inspections, surveys, and consultancy solutions across all vessel types. Our services are precisely tailored to the operational, commercial, and compliance needs of vessel owners, operators, and charterers worldwide.

We go beyond traditional audits and surveys. Our expertise extends to in-depth deficiency analysis, practical corrective action planning, and hands-on close-out support—protecting vessel integrity, strengthening compliance, and optimizing operational performance.

What sets us apart is our owner-aligned, results-driven approach.
We don't simply list findings. We prioritize risks, recommend realistic and cost-effective corrective actions, and actively support closure—helping clients avoid repeat observations, operational delays, off-hire exposure, and unnecessary costs through practical, risk-based solutions.

Our work is guided by a single, uncompromising principle:
Doing the right things, the right way.
This is more than a statement—it is the standard that drives every decision we make, from the bridge to the boardroom. It pushes us to challenge convention, eliminate inefficiencies, and deliver smarter solutions that advance a safer, more efficient, and more sustainable maritime industry.

Our strength lies in people, discipline, and responsiveness. Assignments are led by seasoned professionals—including Master Mariners and Chief Engineers—supported by structured methodologies and consistent documentation standards. With global coverage, 24/7 availability, and rapid report turnaround, VOLAS NexGen Marine delivers clear, decision-ready insights when they matter most.

  • Providing consultative and superintendence expertise throughout the dry-dock process
